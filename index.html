Servings: Undo Add Harvest

Harvest is simple.

  1. To begin, start typing an ingredient and choose one from the list.
  2. Fill in the amount in the units displayed, or select a different unit if available.
  3. Click the "Add Ingredient" button if you need more lines.
  4. Adjust the number of servings, if necessary. The default serving is 1.
  5. Click "Harvest" to see the results. You can adjust the recipe and reharvest anytime.

Some other useful things...

To delete an ingredient, just click the "X" to the right of the item.
To add back ingredients you deleted, just click the "Undo" button.

Saving recipes

You can save a recipe after you've harvested - just scroll down to the bottom of the page. Your saved recipes will appear when you search for ingredients. You can view and manage all your saved recipes in the Recipe Drawer, accessable from the My Recipes bar at the top of the page.

Calories

All food provides some amount of energy the body can use, and that energy is measured in calories. Calories in food can be categorized as originating either from fats, carbohydrates, or protein.

You can learn how your foods and recipes fit into your particular diet by looking at where calories are coming from.
